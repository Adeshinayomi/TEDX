FAQ'S

What is TEDX?

TEDx stands for Technology, Entertainment, and Design. It is a global platform dedicated to sharing “ideas worth spreading.” TEDx events feature inspiring and thought-provoking talks across a wide range of topics—from science and innovation to personal development, creativity, and social impact.

How Can I Get The Tickets?

Go to the event detail section on this webpage click on the plus icon of the ticket you want to purchase after that click on the get ticket button, it is going to take you to a checkout webpage where you will input your details afterwards you will be redirected to another webpage to complete your payments.After a while you will recieve a confirmation email that contains your ticket and a QR code that will be use as a pass for your entrace to the event.

Why did we choose Festac Town?

We chose Festac Town because we believe our ideas deserve a platform—and Festac deserves a place at the table of big ideas. Festac is filled with thinkers, dreamers, builders, and changemakers. TEDxFestac is our way of awakening that spirit and giving our community a voice that can resonate far beyond our borders

Is TEDX Worth My Time

Absolutely. TEDx is a valuable experience because it brings together powerful ideas and inspiring voices that challenge your thinking and expand your perspective. Whether you're looking to develop your communication, creativity, or leadership skills—or simply to be inspired—TEDx offers something truly enriching.
